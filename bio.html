Manju Ashish Buddhaghosh (born on 13th Apr, 1998) is an emerging Indian filmmaker, performer, writer, and director, making their directorial debut with the short film Purnviram. This engaging project is based on their satirical book of the same name, available on Amazon and Flipkart. Purnviram transforms sharp wit into a thrilling narrative, showcasing Buddhaghosh's unique storytelling style.

Hailing from Basti, Uttar Pradesh, and currently residing in Lucknow, Buddhaghosh has a background in IT, working as a Senior UX Engineer and Front-end Developer. Their technical expertise informs their cinematic vision, allowing for compelling visuals and narratives that resonate with audiences.

In addition to filmmaking, Buddhaghosh has an impressive theatre background, having performed for over three years in various productions. They were awarded Best Performer for the portrayal of Akshay Kumar in The Son of Ravana during the 2022 Ramleela in Rohini, Delhi.

Currently, Buddhaghosh is in the process of writing two new projects, Someone Says Men Will Be Men and Kirdar, further expanding their creative portfolio. With a commitment to exploring intricate themes through humor and suspense, Manju Ashish Buddhaghosh is poised to make a significant impact in the film industry.
